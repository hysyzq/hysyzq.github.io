Welcome to the community form collection app! Here's what the markers on the map represent:

  • Green: Actively supports the community through volunteering.
  • Blue: Interested in participating in important meetings only.
  • Gray: Not currently interested in participating.
  • Red: Interested but needs more information.
  • Yellow: No response recorded yet. Please click and fill the form.

Click on a marker to see more details or submit your information via the Google Form.
